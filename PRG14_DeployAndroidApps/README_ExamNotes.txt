MAD Program 14 - Deploying Android Applications

Objective:
- Prepare, sign and publish Android apps to Google Play Store.

Key Concepts:
- APK (Android Package)
- Google Play Developer Console
- App Signing (Keystore)
- Release Channels: Internal, Closed (Alpha), Open (Beta), Production

Procedure:
1. Prepare App for Release
2. Generate Signed APK
3. Setup Google Play Developer Account
4. Upload and Publish App

Registration Fee:
- USD 25 (one-time)

Post Deployment:
- Use ASO (App Store Optimization)
- Promote via social media and online advertising
