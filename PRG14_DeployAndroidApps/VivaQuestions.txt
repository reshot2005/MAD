1. What is the purpose of versionCode?
Answer:
It is an integer used by Google Play to determine if one version is newer than another.

2. Why is a Keystore essential?
Answer:
It contains cryptographic keys used to verify the app's integrity and ensure it has not been tampered with.

3. What are the different release channels?
Answer:
Internal, Closed (Alpha), Open (Beta), and Production.
